Protien Oats

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup milk (or water)
  • 1 scoop protein powder (flavor of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup
  • Optional toppings: sliced fruits, nuts, seeds, or nut butter

Steps to Make:

  1. In a saucepan, combine rolled oats and milk (or water) and bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. Once simmering, reduce heat and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally until oats are cooked and the mixture thickens.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the protein powder and sweetener until well combined.
  4. Transfer to a bowl and add optional toppings such as sliced fruits, nuts, seeds, or nut butter.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy your protein-packed oats!
