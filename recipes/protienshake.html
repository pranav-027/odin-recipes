Protein Shake

Whether its your daily protein requirement or daily calorie goal protein shake comes handy for everything. Anyone with sweet cravings or someone with low appetite the protein shake is a best solution for them. as it gives a lot of calories in very small serving

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop Whey Protein
  • 200 ml Skimmed milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Banana
  • 1 TS Peanut butter
  • Oats

Steps

  1. first grind the oats in mixer and make a powder
  2. Then, Add chopped Banana, milk, peanut butter,ice cubes, adn a scoop of whey in mixer and grind it until its mixed.
  3. Add it in your shaker or glass and have it
