Chicken Sandwich

A delicious mix of mayonnaise, chicken, pepper and some veggies spread on the bread, all done at home in no time! This quick and easy dish is sure to be on your everyday breakfast menu.

Ingredients

  • 150-200gm Boiled Chicken Breast
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • mayonnaise
  • 2 multigrain bread
  • 1 Capsicum
  • 1 lettuce

Steps

  1. Shred the boiled chicken
  2. ad mayonnaise and pepper to it
  3. mix it thoroughly
  4. spread the mixture on bread slices
  5. chop the veggies
  6. out them on slices
  7. put another slice and cut it into small triangles
  8. serve the sandwich with your favorite sauce
